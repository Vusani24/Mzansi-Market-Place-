Mzansi Marketplace Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Mzansi Marketplace is an online platform that connects buyers, sellers, landlords, tenants, and service providers across South Africa. By using this website, you agree to these Terms and Conditions.

Mzansi Marketplace acts as a marketplace platform only and does not own, manufacture, inspect, guarantee, or endorse products, properties, or services listed by users.

2. Marketplace Approval

All item listings, service advertisements, business advertisements, and property listings must be reviewed and approved by Mzansi Marketplace before they become publicly visible.

Mzansi Marketplace reserves the right to approve, reject, edit, suspend, or remove any listing and request additional information before approval.

3. Verification Requirements

Service providers may be required to submit:

  • A valid South African ID, Passport, or recognised identification document.
  • A recent selfie holding the identification document.
  • Supporting documents where applicable.

Items valued above R2,500 may require identity verification, a selfie, and proof of ownership before approval.

4. Buying And Selling

Sellers must provide accurate descriptions, correct pricing, genuine photographs, and honest item conditions.

Buyers are encouraged to inspect items before payment and verify information before completing transactions.

5. Services

Service providers must provide:

  • Full name and surname
  • Service category
  • Contact number
  • Service location
  • Starting price
  • Estimated completion time
  • Portfolio or previous work where available

6. Payments And Charges

Mzansi Marketplace charges a commission of 15% on completed sales and completed services facilitated through the platform.

Accepted payment methods may include:

  • Cash on Delivery (COD)
  • EFT on Delivery
  • Cash Send on Delivery

No delivery means no payment except where a buyer changes their mind while delivery is already in progress. A change-of-mind fee of R30 may apply.

7. Receipts

Mzansi Marketplace may generate receipts for completed transactions for reporting, auditing, moderation, and business performance purposes.

8. Safety Guidelines

Users should inspect products before payment, meet in public places where possible, and report suspicious activity.

9. Prohibited Activities

Users may not sell stolen goods, counterfeit products, engage in scams, or advertise illegal products or services.

10. Limitation Of Liability

Mzansi Marketplace shall not be liable for losses resulting from transactions between users, fraud, poor workmanship, delivery delays, or product defects.

11. Changes To Terms

Mzansi Marketplace may update these Terms and Conditions at any time without prior notice.
