Safety And Verification

Mzansi Market Place is designed for local trust: verified phone numbers, safer handover points, and admin moderation before listings go public.

Public Spaces Only

Meet inside visible malls, transport hubs, police station areas, or service station forecourts. Avoid private homes and isolated locations.

Inspect Before Payment

Buyers should test the item and compare it to the listing before paying. We use cash on delivery, so there is no payment if delivery does not happen.

High-Value Checks

Items above R2,500 can trigger extra seller verification, including RSA ID checks during the full platform phase.
